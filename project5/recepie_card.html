Paneer Butter Masala

Creamy, mildly spiced tomato gravy with paneer cubes.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices

Preparation

Cook tomatoes and spices, blend, add paneer and cream, simmer gently.

Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with tomato, mozzarella, and basil.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Basil leaves

Preparation

Spread sauce on dough, add cheese and basil, then bake until golden.

Mexico - Chicken Enchiladas

Tortillas filled with chicken and topped with spicy red sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded chicken
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cheese

Preparation

Fill tortillas, roll, place in tray, pour sauce, top with cheese, and bake.

Japan - Sushi Rolls

Rice and vegetables wrapped in seaweed with soy dip.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Cucumber, avocado, carrots
  • Rice vinegar, soy sauce

Preparation

Spread rice on nori, add veggies, roll tightly, slice and serve with soy sauce.

France - Ratatouille

A colorful baked dish with layered vegetables and herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes, zucchini, eggplant
  • Bell peppers
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs de Provence

Preparation

Thinly slice vegetables, layer in a dish, drizzle with oil and herbs, bake.
